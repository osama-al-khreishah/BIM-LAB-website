About Us

BIM Lab is a Building Information consultancy located in Amman, Jordan we provide strategic consultancy and planning to Engineering Consultants, contractors and owners to deliver high end drawings and models through technology.

BIM Lab exists where design, building and technology intersect. BIM Lab is an engineering services company we combine the knowledge and experience of architecture, engineering, project management, software engineering and education with a passion for technology to improve the ways of how buildings are designed and constructed.

Building Information as a booming technology and industry has some theoretical principles. BIM Lab helps the institutes to identify, implement and manage this technology and business practice that will enable more effective coordination, communication and collaboration.
